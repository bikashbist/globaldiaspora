Global Diaspora Academy Pvt. Ltd. is a premier educational consultancy dedicated to providing personalized and comprehensive services to students aspiring to pursue higher education abroad. With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and student success, we guide students through every step of their international education journey, ensuring they achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our team of experienced consultants understands the complexities of international education and is dedicated to assisting students in making informed decisions regarding their academic and career aspirations. Whether you're considering undergraduate, graduate, or postgraduate studies, we offer personalized guidance tailored to your unique needs and ambitions.

From selecting the right course and institution to navigating the application process and securing visas, we provide end-to-end assistance to streamline your journey to studying abroad. Additionally, we offer valuable resources and support to help students adapt to new cultures and environments, ensuring a seamless transition into their chosen academic pursuits.

At Global Diaspora Academy, we believe that education has the power to transform lives and shape a brighter future. Global Diaspora Academy offers various services to students from across Nepal, according to their interest and necessity for education in countries: Japan, Australia, Canada, Japan, United States of America, South Korea, New Zealand and the United Kingdom.

Mission

Our mission is to empower students to achieve their academic and career aspirations by providing expert guidance, tailored support, and access to global education opportunities. We strive to bridge the gap between students and world-class educational institutions, fostering a global diaspora of well-educated, culturally aware, and skilled professionals. We believe in optimizing human potential and creating opportunities for personal growth and global understanding through quality international education. We strive to provide best level of assistance to the students in making their dream of studying abroad come true.

Vision

To be the leading educational consultancy recognized for our exceptional services, innovative solutions, and commitment to student success, contributing to the development of globally competitive individuals and a better-educated world.

Core Values

We believe in treating our students with respect and faith. We grow through creativity, invention and innovation. We integrate honesty, integrity and business ethics into all aspects of our business functioning and compliance and our primary goal is to ensure the academic and professional success of our students.
